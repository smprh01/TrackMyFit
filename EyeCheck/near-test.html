Near Vision Test

Please follow the steps below to test your near vision properly:

  1. Wear your glasses or contacts if you have any.
  2. Keep your device at a distance of 14–16 inches (35–40 cm) from your eyes.
  3. Step 1: Cover your right eye and try to read the text below using your left eye.
  4. Step 2: Cover your left eye and read the text again using your right eye.
  5. Ensure you're in a well-lit room for the most accurate results.
